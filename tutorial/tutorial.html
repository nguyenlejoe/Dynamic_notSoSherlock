Objective of the Game

Hey there! In this game you will be playing as legendary detective Sherlock! You'll go through the game using inductive and deductive skills to determine what crimes happened and who committed them. Best of luck!


What is Inductive Reasoning?

Think of it as... determining things from bottom to top! You form an opinion based on something you observe.

For example:
1. All the basketballs I've played with are leather.
2. That means all basketballs are made of leather.

What is Deductive Reasoning?

It's kind of like thinking about things from the top down! You form a question and then argue against it. If there's no arguments to be made, the question is true!

For example:
1. Dogs go woof woof
2. Cherrie goes woof woof
3. Therefore Cherrie is a dog.
